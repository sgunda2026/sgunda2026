History

This Whole website started as just a simple game we always loved to play as kids, just us sisters playing pretend, me as a baker and her as a bartender. Now we are adults we actually continued on this trajectory and now have decided to run this cafe/bar mixture. After taking all legal actions required and hiring friends and other people that share an expertise or at least practice on the stuff we have to do this place was born. We decided at first to create a moon theme which was what me mostly went with but in recent times my sister, Violetta, has had an interest in the "Lost Language of the Angels" as she calls it that also goes by the name of Enochia. So along with another friend of her also interested in the texts, they went off to research more about it. I dont know where she may be right now but she calls and messages me and the family once in a while so we know she is a safe. Now I run the cafe part together with my boyfriend all the while the bar part is run by my sister's husband which in a funny coincidence is my boyfriends younger brother.
